Summary

Artificial intelligence is a tool for detecting synthetic identity fraud. It looks at different pieces of information and finds patterns that might reveal someone is trying to commit fraud. While there are challenges, artificial intelligence can help lenders balance the need to prevent fraud with the need to be fair and trustworthy. It is also important to borrowers because it helps them feel safe,but still has to ensure fairness and transparency. Artificial Intelligence in fraud detection is positive if it helps stop fraud with guarantee in not unfairly targeting some people. The future of fraud detection will involve using artificial intelligence techniques in a way that is fair and protects people's privacy.

Whats the method to cope with the situation?

Use Artificial intelligence!!!

Finding patterns that people or the old systems might miss. AI can Look at how different pieces of identity information are related to how people behave including what devices they use and their credit history. By looking at all these things, artificial intelligence can find anomalies that reveal someone is trying to commit fraud.

Example:

Exciting advancements in technology are helping us understand complex identity patterns! For example, we can now identify if a phone number is linked to different names or if an address appears on multiple applications. Our intelligent systems can also detect email addresses that look algorithm-generated.

Artificial intelligence excels at analyzing behavior, spotting patterns such as quick loan applications or unusual submission times. We consider how forms are filled out, the devices used, and the networks connected to submissions through IP addresses and browser settings.

Credit history is another critical factor. Often, synthetic identities have suspiciously perfect records or manage loans in ways that indicate attempts to build genuine credit. By graphing this information, AI can reveal groups of identities tied to the same individuals, enhancing our systems' security and trustworthiness!

Pros of using AI:

It can reduce debt and fraud losses so that it makes it faster to approve loans and reduce the need for people to review applications by hand. It can also help find fraud rings. By stopping people from getting big loans, artificial intelligence can help banks protect themselves.

Cons of using AI:

It can flag people as bad like new immigrants or young people who are just starting to build credit. Also the privacy concerns as artificial intelligence needs to look at personal information. Besides, it may have bias in the data used to train the intelligence, which leads to unfair treatment of some groups. Nevertheless, there are still always coming up with ways to commit fraud by the public so the artificial intelligence needs to be updated all the time.
